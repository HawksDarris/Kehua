Review Story

Once upon a time, in the beautiful country of France, there was a person named Pierre. Pierre was cheerful and hard-working. He worked at a vineyard on top of a mountain all day and all night, taking care of the grapevines. He was patient and smart, probably the most successful member of his family.
One day, while Pierre was working in the vineyard, he forgot his lunch. He noticed he could smell the delicious fresh-baked bread. Pierre was hungry but either he could finish his work or he could go to the store to buy some food. He decided to be strict about his work and not give up.
Wait a sec... Why can he smell bread? He's in a vineyard.

Pierre asked himself why the grapes smelled like bread.

Oh! It was probably the chemicals I poured on the soil!

Then Pierre realized...

Oh my god! The grapes smell like fresh-baked bread!
The customers will be here in 3 hours! What am I going to do?!
Pierre asked himself

Pierre thought for a couple of minutes and had an idea!

I know! I'll ask my helpful friend, Michel, who is a rich Frenchman from the year 1900 to go to the department store at the bottom of the mountain to buy me wine!

Michel thinks to himself about Pierre's problem.

Of course I will help you, Pierre, I am rich and French and from the 1900s

Pierre's helpful friend, Michel, races down the mountain on his skis. He knows it is the only way to get wine in time for the customers' arrival.

Michel shops at the department store for the most expensive wine because he wants everyone to know how not poor he is.

He buys 50 of the most expensive bottles of excellent wine

Michel ties the wine to his waist and skis back uphill with his wine until he arrives back at Pierre's vineyard

When his helpful friend arrived at the vineyard, Pierre was so happy.

Now he can lie to the customers that this wine came from his bread grapes!

Pierre pours the wine into a big barrel.

I will lie to the customers and tell them I made this wine myself
The customers arrive at the vineyard, ready to taste Pierre's famous wine
Hello customers, I have this wine for you, which I made myself

😏

Pierre's uncle is one of the customers. He is also very rich. He thinks the wine is delicious and he buys the barrel for $100 million.

Write, Draw, Pass

  1. Draw 14 squares: 7 skinny, 7 fat.
  2. Write one sentence in the first skinny box
  3. Pass the paper to the next person.
  4. Draw the sentence from your new paper.
  5. Fold the paper so you can see the drawing, but not the sentence.
  6. Pass the paper.
  7. Write a sentence about the drawing.
