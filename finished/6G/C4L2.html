The Big Tip

This is Charlie.

S/he loves to eat pasta.

Every Friday, Charlie goes to the same pasta restaurant.

Charlie always has the same waiter, Edward.

Charlie likes Edward because Edward never makes a mistake on Charlie's order. Charlie and Edward always chat, and Charlie always leaves $2 as a tip.
