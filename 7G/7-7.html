Your book is wrong about vowels.

A syllable usually has a vowel sound. However, the consonant sounds in bold… [are] syllables too.

(p. 80)

  • We say “litt/ul /” not “litt/le /”.
  • L is a vowel here.
  • (Also the vowel in “pencil” is i )
