Introduction Chain

Let's meet each other

Instructions

  • Introduce the person before you to the next person
  • Introduce yourself; say a fact about yourself
  • Example
    1. My name is Sam and I like to learn languages.

      2. *Next person*

    2. This is Sam. She likes to learn languages. My name is Jeff, and I have two dogs.

      3. *Next person*

    3. This is Jeff. He has two dogs. My name is Jessica and I like reading.
