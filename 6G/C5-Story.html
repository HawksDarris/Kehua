Class Story

Read Quietly

Once upon a time, there was a student named Thea. Thea is in a small house in a forest. One day, Thea wants to see animals in the forest. She sees elephants, lions, cats, and a panda. Oh no! The animals see Thea. The panda sees Pacino in the forest. Thea goes to the forest center and finds a castle. The castle has a river around it. There are crocodiles (!!) in the river! The crocodiles see her and they want to eat her. Thea does not want to be crocodile food. Thea punches the crocodile and runs away.
