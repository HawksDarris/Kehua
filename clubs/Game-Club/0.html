4. Pictionary

4.1. How to play

  1. I give you a word. (我给你一个词。)
  2. No making sounds, no talking. (不发出声音，不说话。)
  3. One person draws the word. (一个人画出这个词。)
  4. Guess the word. (各队猜这个词。)
